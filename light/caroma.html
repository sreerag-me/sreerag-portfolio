CAROMA
Pre-owned Car App

Introduction of CAROMA

CAROMA makes buying a used car simple and stress-free. Browse a wide range of inspected vehicles, compare options, and complete your purchase all from your phone. Our app is designed to be easy to use, so you can find the right car without any hassle.

Task

Develop a mobile application that simplifies the process of buying used cars by offering an intuitive interface, advanced search capabilities, secure transactions, and comprehensive vehicle information.

Solution

CAROMA addresses this task by providing an intuitive platform where users can effortlessly explore a diverse selection of thoroughly inspected used cars. The app incorporates advanced search filters, enabling users to find vehicles based on specific criteria such as brand, price, or body type.
